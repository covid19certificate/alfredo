EN

The present International Health Certificate confirms that the person whose particulars are above:

Has satisfactory state of health.
Has medical exempt from vaccination against Covid-19, has recovered from Covid-19 or has been vaccinated latest 19.11.2021 with Pfizer-Biontech Comirnaty (2nd lot)
